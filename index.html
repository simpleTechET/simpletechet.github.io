EthioSTEM adapts world-class curriculum — Eureka Math, EngageNY — into interactive digital lessons that work for Ethiopian families at home or in community classrooms. No account. No cost. Just open and learn.

Start with Pre-K Math ↗
Research-backed curriculum Lessons follow Eureka Math and EngageNY — proven frameworks adapted with Ethiopian context and household materials.
Concrete → Pictorial → Abstract Every lesson follows the progression research shows works best for young learners: physical first, then visual, then symbolic.
Parent and teacher friendly No training required. Every lesson guides the adult alongside the child, using everyday household materials.
Open and free, always No ads. No accounts. No paywalls. Open the link and learn.
