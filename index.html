💡 Why EthioSTEM?

Built for Ethiopian children,
by an Ethiopian

Research-backed learning that adapts world-class curricula for Ethiopian families — at home or in the classroom.

🎯
Research-Backed
Lessons follow Eureka Math, EngageNY, and NGSS — proven frameworks adapted with Ethiopian context and household materials.
🧱
Concrete → Abstract
Every lesson moves from physical objects to pictures to numbers — the progression research shows works best for young learners.
👨‍👩‍👧
Parent Friendly
No teacher training needed. Every lesson guides the parent alongside the child with simple household materials.
🆓
Free. Always.
No ads. No accounts. No paywalls. Just open any link and start learning — on any phone, tablet, or computer.
